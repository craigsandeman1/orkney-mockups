Our Services

More than wildlife tours

From intimate wildlife encounters to commercial operations, the Scapa Protector is ready.

01

Wildlife Tours

Spot grey and harbour seals, dolphins, porpoises, puffins, and Arctic terns. Trips depart from Kirkwall Harbour every two hours during summer, offering approximately 1.5 hours of exploration on the water.

From £69 per person
02

Commercial Hire

Bespoke boat hire from safety stand-by vessel and fast rescue to inshore film crew support vessel. Full set up for MOB rescue. On call 24 hours a day for emergencies.

Contact for pricing
03

ROV Services

Underwater remotely operated vehicle services for surveys, inspections, and marine research around the Orkney islands.
